# Design Direction — Eazie

## Creative thesis
This site should feel like a **creator portal** instead of a simple profile page.

It needs to communicate:
- artistic confidence
- culture
- momentum
- sonic identity
- premium digital presence

---

## Visual tone
Recommended tone:
- dark luxury
- atmospheric
- sleek
- sharp
- magnetic
- modern

Think:
- premium artist launch page
- elevated creator portfolio
- nightlife / sound / motion energy
- polished but not sterile

---

## Color direction
Because exact brand colors are not confirmed here, build the system with easy-to-update theme tokens.

Suggested default palette direction:
- near-black background
- graphite / charcoal surfaces
- off-white typography
- one vivid accent color
- one soft glow/accent blur

Potential accent categories:
- electric blue
- deep purple
- neon red
- rich amber
- emerald highlight

Use accents sparingly. Premium means restraint.

---

## Typography direction
Use strong hierarchy.

Suggested pairing approach:
- bold display font for headings
- clean modern sans-serif for body and UI

Typography should communicate:
- confidence
- rhythm
- clarity
- polish

Avoid:
- novelty fonts
- overly compressed unreadable headers
- too many font families

---

## Motion direction
Motion should support identity, not distract.

Recommended:
- fade + blur reveals
- smooth scaling panels
- ambient background drift
- subtle parallax layers
- card hover lift
- CTA glow response
- transition from Bio Mode to Full Experience via expansion and staged reveal

Avoid:
- chaotic bouncing
- excessive rotation
- gimmicky effects
- long delays before interaction

---

## Layout principles
- mobile-first
- strong spacing rhythm
- obvious hierarchy
- each section earns its place
- one visual focus per section
- no overcrowded link stacks

---

## Hero direction
The hero should feel like a statement.

Potential features:
- artist portrait
- background gradient or moody visual texture
- subtle animated light wash
- concise but hard-hitting copy
- strong paired CTAs

---

## Bio Mode direction
Bio Mode should feel like:
- premium social landing page
- fast access
- polished identity
- zero confusion

This is not just a placeholder. It must stand on its own.

---

## Music section direction
Spotify embeds can look plain by default, so surround them with:
- premium card framing
- supporting labels
- release context
- spacing and visual hierarchy

Make music feel central, not buried.

---

## Mobile experience
This experience must shine on mobile.

Priorities:
- frictionless scroll
- fast decision-making
- thumb-zone CTAs
- no tiny text
- sticky action bar when helpful

---

## Finish quality checklist
- consistent corner radius
- consistent spacing scale
- polished transitions
- strong empty space usage
- deliberate emphasis
- no section feeling “template-ish”
